Introduction:

Kid’s Location System is a cloud computing system basing on original cameras in public area such as department store, market and attractions. The system is not just a camera, but it can also geo tagging any kids you took. Cooperating with IP cameras in public, Pointing children location immediately. Users can find kid’s location as soon as possible when the kids get lost. According to the research from Crime Against Children research center show that children who were reported missing (i.e., reported to the police or a missing children's agency in order to locate them) was estimated to be 797,500, which is equivalent to a rate of 11.4 children per 1,000 in the U.S. population. In Taiwan, children missing always happen in department store, market and amusement park. The percentage of children missing in department is 17.3 percent, where is the most children losing happened.
